The Organic Waste Management project is a comprehensive web-based solution designed to tackle the issue of organic waste by turning it 

into valuable resources.Developed using HTML, CSS, and other web technologies, this project focuses on reducing waste and promoting 

sustainability by converting organic materials into biogas and compost. Our platform not only educates users on how to produce biogas 

and compost at home but also provides an online marketplace for eco-friendly products.Users can buy environmentally friendly products 

directly from our site, encouraging a green lifestyle. Additionally, the platform offers a unique feature that connects sellers and buyers 

of organic waste, fostering a community that supports waste reduction and reuse. Join us in making a positive impact on the environment! 

Explore our site to learn, shop, and contribute to a sustainable future.
